Personal Training

One on One Personal Training is at its best when done here at Studio One Fitness. We believe in the overall well being and physical development of all our members in a fun, comfortable, and personalized atmosphere. Our clients are our friends and together we build a relationship of trust and mutual respect. The goals that we set are for our clients and us to reach together in an efficient, safe, and fun manner. Our goal in One on One Personal Training is to motivate and educate our clients as they work towards their personal goals, while continually setting new goals and standards for the future. We will provide our clients with all the necessary tools for them to succeed from the day they start. Providing them with written exercises programs, regular nutritional counseling, cardiovascular programs, quarterly progression assessments, to lifestyle coaching not only in the studio but outside communication throughout. Our clients become part of our lives, as we become part of theirs. Together, we work as a team and stay as a team.
