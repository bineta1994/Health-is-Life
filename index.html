    Services

    Personal Training

    One on One Personal Training is at its best when done here at Studio One Fitness. We believe in the overall well being and physical development of all our members in a fun, comfortable, and personalized atmosphere. Our clients are our friends and together we build a relationship of trust and mutual respect. The goals that we set are for our clients and us to reach together in an efficient, safe, and fun manner. Our goal in One on One Personal Training is to motivate and educate our clients as they work towards their personal goals, while continually setting new goals and standards for the future. We will provide our clients with all the necessary tools for them to succeed from the day they start. Providing them with written exercises programs, regular nutritional counseling, cardiovascular programs, quarterly progression assessments, to lifestyle coaching not only in the studio but outside communication throughout. Our clients become part of our lives, as we become part of theirs. Together, we work as a team and stay as a team.

    Physio-Med

    Physiotherapy, Chiropractic, and/or Massage Therapy In House Here at Studio One Fitness, we offer a rare service that most fitness institutes do not have. We have partnered up with several Physio-Med clinics in Mississauga to help better serve our clients. We now offer any type of physiotherapy, chiropractic, and/or massage therapy in house here at the studio. We have programs such as Pain and Injury Recovery programs, Metabolic and Chronic Condition Management programs, Sports Specific and Performance based programs, to general Health and Wellness programs. Together with the staff of Physio-Med and the staff here at Studio One Fitness, we bring together a well rounded fitness program supervised by Professionals for you.

    Meal Prep

    When it comes to a healthy lifestyle, choices you make and food preparation is the key to you, r success. Studies have suggested that those who spend time on meal preparation have healthier dietary habits. Here at Studio One Fitness, our meal preparation has been designed by you the client to ensure that not only do you enjoy what you eat, but the results are there when you are finished. You might be asking what we do different from all other meal preparation companies? We can tell you here at Studio One Fitness, our clients have helped with the process of how we do meal prep here. We have taken catering to your needs to a whole new level when it comes to meal preparation. From specific foods of each meal, how it is seasoned, the type of meat and vegetable quality, down to how many exact meals required per week. Unlike other companies that demand a minimum order of meals per week or month, here at Studio One Fitness we do it on a weekly basis with no commitment to a minimum order of meals.

    We literally cater to you and your lifestyle, whether you only need breakfasts because your in too much of a rush to prepare it each day, or down to only one day a week because that day is full of meetings each week and you can never get your meals in. We consult on a weekly basis with our clients to discuss the clients needs for the upcoming week, and not only can you order food for specific fitness goals, but you can just because you have no time to prepare. As simple as that, you can contact us today for more details!
